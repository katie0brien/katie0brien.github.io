About

Ocatvian Winner of BBC Sound 2019, French-born, south-east London-raised rapper Octavian announced himself to the wider world with the release of his first official single, 'Party Here' in 2017. The single not only showcased the 21 year old rapper's distinctive flow and personality, but his ability to bring together the many genres of London's fractured music scene. His music takes elements of R&B, Dancehall, grime and house - a truly unique sound that is setting the template for rap in 2018.
